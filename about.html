About Me

I am an Assistant Lecturer in the Department of Statistics and Actuarial Sciences at JKUAT and a Ph.D. candidate in Mathematics at Sokoine University of Agriculture (2021–2025). My research and teaching focus on applied statistics, mathematical modelling, and data science for human and animal health, food systems, and policy applications.

Objective

To contribute to academic excellence, research innovation, and capacity building in Statistics, Applied Mathematics, and Data Science, with emphasis on sustainable development and evidence-based policymaking.

Research & Teaching Interests

  • Mathematical Modelling & Statistical Inference
  • Machine Learning & Data Science
  • Time Series Analysis & Forecasting
  • Spatial Statistics & Epidemiological Modelling
  • Applications in Public Health and Agriculture
